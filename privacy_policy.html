Privacy Policy for FlyBook

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

At FlyBook, accessible from our mobile application, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by FlyBook and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you:

  • Personal Data: Name, Email address, Phone number.
  • Location Data: We may use and store information about your location if you give us permission to do so. We use this data to provide features of our Service, to improve and customize our Service.
  • Media: Photos and videos from your device for profile management and marketplace listings.

2. Use of Data

FlyBook uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service
  • To notify you about changes to our Service
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features
  • To provide customer support
  • To monitor the usage of the Service
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues

3. Permissions (Android)

To provide a full experience, FlyBook requests access to:

  • Camera: For taking profile pictures and scanning.
  • Microphone: For audio/video calling features.
  • Location: To show relevant books and jobs near you.
  • Overlay: To show incoming call screens when the app is in background.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@flybook.com.
